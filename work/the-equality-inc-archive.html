The Equality Inc. Archive
★ ⊹₊ ⋆ Editorial design
The Equality Inc. Archive is a place to celebrate the history and portrayal of the LGBTQIA+ community, through a collection of curated content, including articles, interviews, photographs, and personal narratives. This project is dedicated to preserving and honoring the diverse stories, struggles, and triumphs of LGBTQIA+ individuals throughout history. Through a collection of curated content, including articles, interviews, photographs, and personal narratives, I want to showcase the various ways in which LGBTQIA+ individuals have shaped our world and advanced the cause of equality and acceptance. Whether it’s highlighting key moments in LGBTQIA+ history, profiling influential figures, or documenting grassroots movements, our archive is a living tribute to the ongoing struggle for LGBTQIA+ rights and recognition.
